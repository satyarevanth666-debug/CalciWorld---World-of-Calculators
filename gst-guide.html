GST Calculator Guide

The GST calculator helps you add or remove GST from a price quickly. It is useful for small business pricing, invoice review, and understanding the tax component of purchases.

How it works

You can choose whether to add GST to a base amount or remove GST from a price that already includes tax.

Formula used

When adding GST:

GST = Amount × GST % ÷ 100
Total = Amount + GST

When removing GST:

Base = Total ÷ (1 + GST % ÷ 100)
GST = Total − Base

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Enter the amount or total price.
  2. Select whether you are adding or removing GST.
  3. Enter the GST percentage.
  4. Calculate to see the final output.

Example

Price = ₹1,000, GST = 18%

GST = ₹180, Total = ₹1,180.

When to use it

  • Prepare invoices with correct tax calculation.
  • Check whether a given price includes GST.
  • Estimate final costs for purchases and services.
