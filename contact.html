Contact CalciWorld

We welcome questions, suggestions, and bug reports from all CalciWorld visitors. Your feedback helps us improve the calculators and build better guides over time.

Email

Send messages to satyarevanth666@gmail.com. Please include the name of the calculator or guide you are referring to, along with a few details about the issue.

Project suggestions

If you'd like to request a new calculator or a new finance/math guide, email us with the topic and why it's useful. We prioritize tools that solve real problems and help people make better decisions.

Bug reports

To report a bug, include the calculator name, the values you entered, and the result you expected. If possible, attach a screenshot of the page.

Privacy and support

We do not collect personal data from visitors, and we treat every question with care. You can also find answers on our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions pages.

Follow-up

We typically respond within a few business days, but response times may vary. If you need a fast answer, share the most important information up front.
