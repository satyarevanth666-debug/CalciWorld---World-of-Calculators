GST Calculator Guide

Use the GST calculator to add or remove Goods and Services Tax from a price. This guide helps you understand the tax component, invoice values, and how to compare tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive amounts.

How it works

You can choose whether you are calculating GST on a base amount or extracting the base amount from a price that already includes GST.

Formula used

When adding GST:

GST = Amount × GST % ÷ 100
Total = Amount + GST

When removing GST:

Base = Total ÷ (1 + GST % ÷ 100)
GST = Total − Base

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Enter the original amount or total price.
  2. Select whether the GST is being added or removed.
  3. Enter the GST percentage.
  4. Click calculate to see the results.

Example

Original amount = ₹1,000, GST = 18%

GST = ₹180 and Total = ₹1,180.

Use cases

  • Prepare invoices with correct tax calculation.
  • Verify whether a price already includes GST.
  • Compare retail prices across products or services.
