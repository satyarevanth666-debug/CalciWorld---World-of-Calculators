Privacy Policy

At CalciWorld, we are committed to protecting your privacy while delivering a fast and useful calculator experience. This policy explains what data we use, how it is stored, and how we keep your browsing and calculations private.

No Personal Data Collection

CalciWorld does not collect personal data, such as names, email addresses, or payment information. The calculators run locally in your browser, and the site does not send your calculation inputs or results to any external servers.

Local Storage

To improve your experience, CalciWorld may store the following in your browser:

  • Theme choice (light or dark mode)
  • Favorite calculators selections
  • Recent calculators and recent results

All of this data is stored in your browser's local storage and is only accessible on this device.

No Tracking or Third-Party Analytics

CalciWorld does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services. We do not track your browsing behavior across other websites.

External Links

Some pages may include links to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. If you follow an external link, review that site’s privacy policy separately.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy over time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the most recent version will always be available here.
