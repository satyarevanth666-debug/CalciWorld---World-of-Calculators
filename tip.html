Tip Calculator Guide

The tip calculator helps you split a restaurant bill fairly and figure out how much each person should pay. It is ideal for groups, shared rides, or any situation where you want to add a tip and divide the total cost.

What this calculator does

This calculator takes the total bill amount, the tip percentage, and the number of people sharing the bill. It then calculates the tip amount, the final total, and the cost per person.

Formula used

The main calculations are:

Tip = bill × tip% ÷ 100
Total = bill + tip
Per person = Total ÷ people

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Enter the total bill amount.
  2. Enter the tip percentage you want to leave.
  3. Enter the number of people sharing the bill.
  4. Click calculate to see the tip and per-person amounts.

Use cases

  • Split restaurant bills evenly among friends.
  • Calculate tipping amounts at cafes or rideshares.
  • Compare different tip percentages before choosing one.
  • Check the effect of a larger tip on the total cost.

FAQs

What tip percentage should I use?

Common tip rates are 10%–20%, depending on local custom and service quality. Use the calculator to compare values and choose what feels fair.

Should I round the per-person amount?

Rounding is optional. You can round each person’s share to the nearest rupee and adjust one person’s payment slightly if needed.

Does this include tax?

If your bill already includes tax, just use the total bill amount. If tax is separate, add it to the bill before calculating the tip.

How to use this page

Use the tip calculator for quick bill-splitting decisions. Try different tip percentages to see how they affect the per-person total.
