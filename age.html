Age Calculator Guide

The age calculator helps you determine exactly how old someone is in years, months, and days. It is useful for planning birthdays, verifying identity documents, and setting milestones.

How it works

Enter a date of birth, and the calculator computes the interval between that date and today. It accounts for calendar months and days to give you a precise age.

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Select the birth date using the date picker.
  2. Review the result to see years, months, and leftover days.
  3. Use the result for planning and record-keeping.

Example

If someone was born on 1 January 2000, and today is 14 June 2026, the calculator shows 26 years, 5 months, and 13 days.

When to use it

  • Verify age for official forms and applications.
  • Plan birthday milestones and anniversaries.
  • Compare dates for age-based eligibility or benefits.

FAQs

Will it work for future birth dates?

It expects a past date. If you enter a future date, it will show a negative or invalid age.

Is this age useful for legal documents?

This tool provides an exact duration but should not replace official documentation for legal proceedings.
