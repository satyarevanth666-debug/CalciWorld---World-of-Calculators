Income Tax Calculator Guide

The tax calculator helps you estimate your annual income tax liability using a simple progressive slab model. It is useful for planning take-home pay, budgeting, and evaluating salary offers.

How it works

Enter your annual income, and the calculator applies common tax slabs to estimate total tax and net income.

Formula used

The calculator applies rates in brackets, for example:

  • 0% for income up to ₹250,000
  • 5% for the next ₹250,000
  • 20% for the next ₹500,000
  • 30% for income above ₹1,000,000

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Enter your expected annual income.
  2. Click calculate to see estimated tax and net income.
  3. Use this estimate for salary planning and saving goals.

Example

Annual income = ₹8,00,000

The calculator estimates tax using the slab rates and shows the remaining net income.

Use cases

  • Estimate your monthly take-home pay.
  • Compare salary offers before accepting a job.
  • Plan savings and investments around your expected tax burden.
